# 03 — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## How this document is structured

Features are grouped by **release milestone** (MVP → V1 → V2). Each feature has a stable ID (e.g. `F-AUTH-001`) so that engineering tickets, tests, and AI-agent tasks can reference it unambiguously. This document is the source of truth for *what* gets built; the technical and AI-agent docs cover *how*.

---

## Glossary

- **Player** — end consumer using Echo.
- **Vignette** — a single decision moment inside a playthrough.
- **Act** — a thematic block of vignettes (Morning, Midday, Afternoon, Evening).
- **Playthrough** — one complete journey through a Season (typically 20–30 minutes).
- **Season** — a complete narrative arc consisting of 4 acts and 15–25 vignettes, releasing on a content cadence (target: every 6–10 weeks).
- **Portrait** — the generative visual + textual output produced at the end of a playthrough.
- **Trait Vector** — the underlying multi-dimensional personality score that drives the Portrait.
- **Institution** — a B2B customer (school, clinic, coaching practice).
- **Seat** — one institutional player slot, billed monthly.

## MVP — Feature scope

### F-CORE-001 — Single-Season playthrough engine
**Description.** Player can begin and complete one full Season (4 acts, 15–25 vignettes) in a single sitting or across multiple sittings.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Player can start, pause, resume, and complete a playthrough.
- Progress is persisted locally and synced to backend (when authenticated) within 5 seconds of any choice.
- Player can never accidentally skip a vignette by tapping past it; all transitions are deliberate.
- No timed pressure — the player can sit on any vignette indefinitely.

### F-CORE-002 — Vignette renderer
**Description.** Each vignette is a screen presenting a short narrative beat (text, optional ambient art, optional sound) followed by 2–4 choices.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Renders text at multiple lengths gracefully (mobile portrait, tablet, desktop window).
- Choices are presented as natural-language options, not labeled archetypes.
- Choice timing, hesitation, and revisits are all logged as part of the input signal (not just the final selection).
- Optional ambient audio loops smoothly across vignette transitions.

### F-CORE-003 — Trait scoring engine (rule-based v1)
**Description.** Every player input feeds a trait vector mapped to Big Five (OCEAN), Schwartz values (10 dimensions), and attachment style proxies. V1 uses transparent rule-based weights authored alongside vignettes.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Each choice in a vignette carries a tagged weighted contribution to one or more trait dimensions.
- Trait vector is computed deterministically from the full set of choices.
- All scoring is auditable: a developer/researcher tool can reproduce the trait vector from the event log.
- Trait scoring runs server-side after playthrough completion (not client-side).

### F-CORE-004 — Portrait generation
**Description.** At playthrough completion, the trait vector is used to generate (a) a unique procedural visual Portrait and (b) a short prose reflection (3–5 sentences).
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Visual generation is procedural / parametric (deterministic from trait vector + seed) — *not* purely AI-image-generated in MVP, for cost, latency, and brand-consistency reasons. (AI image gen optional in V2 as an enhancement.)
- Prose reflection is generated by an LLM call from a tightly templated prompt; output is filtered through a safety and tone classifier before display.
- End-to-end generation completes within 6 seconds of playthrough completion under normal load.
- Portrait is saved server-side and assigned a permanent shareable URL.

### F-AUTH-001 — Account creation (light)
**Description.** Player can complete a playthrough without an account. To save the Portrait, sync across devices, or share it, they create a light account.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Anonymous playthrough is supported end-to-end.
- Sign-up requires email OR Apple/Google OAuth.
- Age gate at sign-up: under-13 blocked entirely; 13–17 routed into the youth-safe flow (see `F-SAFE-001`).
- Password reset, email change, and account deletion all functional.

### F-SAFE-001 — Youth-safe flow
**Description.** Players aged 13–17 are routed into a flow with stricter defaults: no public sharing, no friend comparison without verified parent/guardian consent, additional content filter on prose generation.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Age input at sign-up is required and not bypassable.
- Under-13 are blocked with a friendly message; their data is not stored.
- 13–17 receive limited features by default; expanded features require verified guardian email consent flow.
- All under-18 accounts have stricter prose generation guardrails (configurable in `F-LLM-001`).

### F-SHARE-001 — Portrait sharing
**Description.** Player can share their Portrait via system share sheet to social platforms, or via a public web page.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Shared image is high-resolution (1080×1920 for stories, 1080×1080 for square).
- Web Portrait page renders on any browser, includes a CTA to download Echo.
- Sharing is opt-in; never default on. Youth-safe flow has sharing disabled by default.

### F-SETTINGS-001 — Settings and account management
**Description.** Standard account management surface.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Notification controls (Echo's default is *off*; opt-in only).
- Data export (GDPR right to portability) — user receives JSON within 72 hours.
- Account deletion (GDPR right to erasure) — completes within 30 days, hard-deletes Portraits, redacts event logs to anonymized aggregate.

## V1 — Public Launch additional scope

### F-CORE-005 — Desktop builds (Windows + macOS)
**Description.** Bring the same playthrough experience to Windows and macOS via the shared codebase.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Windows 10+ and macOS 12+ supported.
- Window resize and full-screen handled.
- Keyboard and mouse input feel native, not ported.
- Code-signed and notarized for both platforms.

### F-CORE-006 — Second Season
**Description.** Additional 4-act season with a new protagonist and narrative arc.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Vignettes pass the same review pipeline as Season 1.
- Trait weights re-calibrated against Season 1 baseline so cross-season Trait Vectors remain comparable.

### F-SOCIAL-001 — Friend comparison
**Description.** Two consenting users who have both completed the same Season can view a side-by-side comparison of their Portraits, with one specific "divergence moment" highlighted.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Both users must explicitly opt into the comparison.
- Comparison shows Portraits side-by-side and one carefully chosen vignette where their choices differed.
- Comparison does not show raw choices for any other vignette.
- Comparison generates a single shareable artifact.
- Youth-safe accounts cannot participate without verified guardian consent.

### F-MONEY-001 — Free tier + Echo+ premium
**Description.** Free tier includes one Season per quarter; Echo+ unlocks the full library, archival of all Portraits, deeper reflection content, and early access to new Seasons.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Subscription billed via Apple IAP, Google Play Billing, and Stripe on desktop.
- Single subscription tier (Echo+) priced approximately €4.99/month or €39.99/year (final pricing per `09_Monetization`).
- Cancellation and refund flows functional and compliant with each store's rules.

### F-LLM-001 — Reflection generation pipeline
**Description.** Productionize prose-reflection generation with safety, tone, and brand-voice guardrails.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Two-stage pipeline: structured prompt with trait vector + retrieval of brand-voice exemplars → generation → post-generation safety classifier → display.
- Safety classifier filters: no clinical diagnoses, no suggestive content for under-18 accounts, no language interpretable as self-harm trigger.
- Tone classifier ensures generosity and specificity, blocks generic outputs.
- All generations logged with prompt + output for review.
- Manual override flow: any flagged output is replaced with a curated fallback reflection and reported to a review queue.

## V2 — Institutional scope

### F-B2B-001 — Institution onboarding
**Description.** Institutional admin can create an institution, invite educators/coaches, allocate seats, and configure youth-safe defaults.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Institution admin creates org, sets domain allowlist for member emails.
- Admin invites members (educators/coaches), assigns them sub-cohorts.
- Members invite players (students/clients) — invitation includes age verification and, for under-18, guardian consent flow.

### F-B2B-002 — Institution dashboard
**Description.** Educator/coach dashboard showing cohort-level aggregate trait distributions and individual Portraits only with player consent.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Aggregate cohort view: trait distributions, never identifying individuals.
- Individual view: only Portrait + optional player notes; raw choices never exposed.
- Players see exactly what their educator/coach sees about them, at any time.
- Export to PDF for parent-conference or coaching-session use.

### F-B2B-003 — Seat-based billing
**Description.** Per-seat monthly billing for institutions.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- Stripe-backed invoicing.
- Tiered pricing: Schools, Coaching practices, Clinical practices — see `09_Monetization`.
- Annual contract option with discount.
- Self-serve and sales-assisted onboarding paths.

### F-COMPLIANCE-001 — Institutional compliance posture
**Description.** Documentation and infrastructure to support institutional sales: DPA, sub-processor list, security questionnaire responses, SOC 2 Type I underway.
**Acceptance criteria.**
- DPA template ready for signature.
- Sub-processor list public on the site.
- Security questionnaire (CAIQ or similar) completed.
- SOC 2 Type I audit initiated.

## V2.5 and beyond — directional, not committed

- **Same Vignette Live** — opt-in, weekly featured vignette that thousands of users play at once; results show aggregate "the world's choices" and your own.
- **Educator content packs** — discussion guides tied to Echo Seasons for classroom and coaching use.
- **Localization** — French, German, Spanish, then Portuguese and Italian; voice-acted ambient layers where possible.
- **Longitudinal Portraits** — for players who play multiple Seasons over time, a quietly evolving "how you've changed" surface, gated behind very strong consent.

## Out-of-scope (deliberate, indefinitely)

- Real-time multiplayer gameplay
- User-generated vignettes
- In-app messaging or social feed
- Avatars or profile customization beyond the Portrait
- Ad-supported tier
- Crypto, NFTs, "owning your personality on-chain," and anything similar

These are out of scope to protect the brand soul described in `01_Product_Vision`.

## Non-functional requirements (apply across all features)

- **Performance.** Cold start under 3 seconds on a mid-range Android device (Pixel 6a class). Vignette-to-vignette transition under 200 ms.
- **Offline.** A playthrough started online can be completed entirely offline; choices sync when connectivity returns.
- **Accessibility.** All text meets WCAG AA contrast on default theme. Full screen-reader support on iOS and Android from V1. Dyslexia-friendly font option.
- **Privacy by default.** Telemetry is opt-in for under-18; opt-out for adults but defaulted to a minimal "essential only" set.
- **Internationalization.** All player-facing strings externalized; LLM prose generation operates per locale once localized.
- **Auditability.** All trait-relevant events are immutable and reproducible. Researcher tooling can replay any playthrough deterministically.
